Welcome to Casa Da Vinci

Where Italian elegance meets Mediterranean paradise

Nestled in the charming coastal town of Badesi, our Leonardo da Vinci Residence offers an unparalleled vacation experience. This beautifully appointed apartment combines traditional Sardinian architecture with modern luxury amenities.

Wake up to breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea, enjoy breakfast on your private terrace, and spend your days exploring pristine beaches just minutes away. Our residence is designed to provide the perfect backdrop for your Sardinian adventure.
