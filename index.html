Our Insider Guide to Badesi

Discover the best of North Sardinia with our local recommendations

🏖️ Best Beaches Nearby

1. Li Junchi Beach

2.8 Km – Crystal clear waters, white sand, perfect for families. Our #1 recommendation!

2. Badesi Marina Beach

3.5 Km – Long sandy beach, great for walking, water sports, and beach bars.

3. Baia delle Mimose

3 Km – Quiet, family-friendly beach next to Li Junchi, shallow waters ideal for children.

4. Isola Rossa Beach

15 Km – Stunning red cliffs, great for snorkeling, amazing sunset views.

5. Costa Paradiso

22 Km – Hidden paradise with dramatic rock formations, less crowded.

🍝 Restaurants & Shops Nearby

1. InForno Pizzeria

150m walking distance – Excellent wood-fired pizza, casual atmosphere, perfect for quick dinners!

2. Gelatomania

200m walking distance – Amazing Italian gelato (ice cream)! Must-try after dinner!

3. Local Supermarket

100m walking distance – Grocery store for your daily needs, fresh bread, local products.

4. Ristorante L'Ancora

Badesi Marina, 4 Km – Fresh seafood, waterfront dining. Try the lobster pasta!

5. Agriturismo Li Lioni

8 Km – Authentic Sardinian farm-to-table experience, amazing local dishes.

🍷 Top 3 Wine Tastings

1. Cantina Giogantinu

12 Km – Award-winning Vermentino wines, beautiful vineyard tours with tastings.

2. Tenute Dettori

35 Km – Organic biodynamic wines, unique experience, book in advance!

3. Cantina del Vermentino

25 Km, Monti – Traditional Gallura wines, family-run winery with passion.

💡 Insider Tips

  • Best time to visit beaches: Early morning (7-9 AM) or late afternoon (5-7 PM) for fewer crowds
  • Grocery shopping: Local market in Badesi every Saturday morning – fresh produce & local specialties
  • Day trips: Visit Castelsardo (35 min), Santa Teresa di Gallura (50 min), or La Maddalena archipelago (1h 30min)
  • Hidden gem: Valle della Luna (Moon Valley) near Santa Teresa – magical rock formations, perfect for sunset
